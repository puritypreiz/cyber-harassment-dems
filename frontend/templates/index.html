A safe place to document and report cyber harassment

This system helps students securely capture evidence of online harassment, track their case, and connect with support — with encryption, tamper-evident audit trails, and QR-verified evidence at every step.

Get started Log in

How it works

  1. Create a confidential (optionally anonymous) account.
  2. Open a case and upload evidence — files are encrypted (AES-256) and hashed (SHA-256) on upload.
  3. Each evidence item gets a signed QR code proving its integrity for chain-of-custody purposes.
  4. A counselor is assigned automatically; legal staff can be looped in for serious cases.
